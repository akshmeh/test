Food Services Products

AGRO CHEMICALS

Producing superior products that meet worldwide standards.

Agro Chemicals was set up to manufacture power alcohol from sugar cane molasses for mixing with petrol to produce various blends of gasohol.

Horticulture & Floriculture

Reaching markets all over the world.

Financial Services

Sports & Entertainment

Pioneers in the Indian domestic T20 circketing history.

International Trade

Commanding the Trade Winds across the Seven Seas.

Established in 1940, Mehta Private Ltd. is traditionally a garment company, renowned for fabrics and readymade garments. It is now also engaged in exporting commodities like fly ash, engineering goods, fashion accessories and corporate gifts.

Sugar

Inclusive growth for a better society.

Sugar Corporation of Uganda Limited plays a key role in the development of the Ugandan economy. It boasts of a full-fledged township, regular in-house training programs, and scholarships for study in universities for its employees.

Electrical Cables

Manufacturing quality cables with certification to ISO 9001 2000 specifications.

A pioneer unit in Uganda, the cable Corporation Limited manufactures all types of cables. It holds the distinction of being the only cable manufacturing company in the region certified by the Uganda National bureau of Standards.

Engineering

Building a better world, a step at a time.

The Ugma Engineering Corporation limited is run as a joint venture between the Government of Uganda and the Mehta Group. IT is one of the largest engineering corporations in East Africa and manufactures spare parts for a wide range of industries.

Snowcem Paints

Saurashtra Cement Limited - Paint Division.

‘Snowcem’ brand has been acquired by The Mehta Group’s ‘Saurashtra Cement Limited’. Brand Snowcem is now part of Saurashtra Cement Limited - Paint Division.
